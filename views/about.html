Who I Am

Hi! I'm Rachel, a full-stack developer with a passion for creating intuitive and beautiful web applications. With over 5 years of experience in web development, I specialize in building responsive and user-friendly interfaces that make cooking and recipe management a joy.

My journey into web development started with a love for both cooking and coding. This recipe portal is a perfect blend of these passions, designed to help food enthusiasts discover and organize their favorite recipes.

This recipe portal was created with the goal of making cooking more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. By combining clean design with powerful functionality, I aimed to create a platform that's both beautiful and practical.

Future plans include adding more interactive features, a community section, and personalized recipe recommendations.
